The players will battle it out in front of college recruiters for their share of a $1000 cash prize.

Spring 2022: Week 1 Recap

William Jackson

Rocket League

On Monday, Rocket league kicked off the season with a great performance, defeating Utah State University Eastern in a convincing 3-0 sweep. After a slow start that saw game one tied 2-2 with 3:00 remaining, The Jaguars pulled ahead and never looked back.

Game scores:
Game 1: 6-2
Game 2: 10-0
Game 3: 6-1

Rainbow Six

For their season opener, rainbow six faced off against the Hocking College Hawks. JCC took a tough 1-2 loss.

Smash

JCC Smash had a mixed bag of results for their opening week. Jonathan and Justin both Swept, while Lamont and Ty both lost their respective matches. Daniel's opponent no-showed the match and he was given a win by Forefeit. Nick was unable to make his game and took an FF loss.

Overwatch

JCC Overwatch swept Grays Harbor College in the final game of week 1. Overwatch was hot from the start and never looked back, taking Lijang Tower 2-0, Hollywood 1-0, and then Hanamura 1-0 to close out the match. The Jaguars' defense held strong on the final two maps, not giving up a single objective.
