Jaguar Esports

Founded in 2021, the JCC Jaguars have over 20 student-athletes competing in 4 major titles: Rocket League, Overwatch, Super Smash Bros., and Rainbow Six Siege. The campus features a gaming room with 6 high end Alienware PCs, Two TVs, a separate announcer's booth, and (most importantly) rainbow LED lighting. The Jaguars compete in the NJCAA, fielding strong teams in all four games. The spring 2022 season begins February 14th! Lets go Jags!
